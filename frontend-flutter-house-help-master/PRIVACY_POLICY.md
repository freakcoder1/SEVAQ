# SevaQ Customer App Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: April 27, 2026  
**Last Updated**: April 27, 2026

## 1. Introduction
SevaQ ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Name, email address, phone number
- Home address and service location
- Profile photo (optional)
- Payment information (processed via Razorpay, we do not store card details)

### Usage Information
- Service booking history
- App usage analytics
- Device information (model, OS version)
- IP address and network information

### Location Information
- Precise GPS location (when using the App to find nearby workers)
- Coarse location (for service area verification)
- Background location (only when necessary for active service tracking)

### Firebase & Third-Party Data
- Firebase Authentication: User ID, login credentials
- Firebase Cloud Messaging: Device tokens for push notifications
- Google Maps: Location data for service address verification

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- To create and manage your account
- To connect you with verified service workers
- To process bookings and payments
- To send booking confirmations, reminders, and updates
- To improve our services and user experience
- To comply with legal obligations

## 4. Information Sharing
We share your information only with:
- **Service Workers**: Name, address, and contact info necessary to provide services
- **Payment Processors**: Razorpay for secure payment processing
- **Service Providers**: Firebase (hosting, auth, notifications), Google Maps (location services)
- **Legal Authorities**: When required by law or to protect our rights

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

## 5. Data Security
- All data transmission uses TLS/SSL encryption
- Firebase services comply with SOC 2, ISO 27001 security standards
- Payment data is handled by PCI-DSS compliant Razorpay
- Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only

## 6. Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Opt-out of non-essential notifications
- Withdraw location permissions via device settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@sevaq.com

## 7. Children's Privacy
Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us immediately.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-App notification.
